TalkQuill — Support

TalkQuill turns your voice into text, privately and on-device.

Getting started

Tap the microphone, speak, then tap again to stop. Your text appears on screen to copy or share.

AI polish (optional)

To clean up your dictation with Claude, open Settings (the gear icon) and paste your own Anthropic API key. Without a key, TalkQuill works fully on-device with no network access.
